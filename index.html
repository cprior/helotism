About Me

Although introduced to early computers as a learning method in primary school I was never involved with them when growing up. Only after school I got interested in "how does spiegel.de function"? This led to exhaustive knowledge in the creation of dynamic webpages. The Helotism project is also helping me to catch up with what has changed since about 2010.

As a business guy I see so much potential in analysing data not just to generate data-about-data, but also to improve real physical products.
